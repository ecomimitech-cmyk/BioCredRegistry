# BioCred Landing Page Spec v1.0

**Source:** Alexander Funk, March 2 2026  
**Domain:** biocredregistry.com

---

## Three Goals

1. **Establish legitimacy** — BioCred is a credible, evidence-based registry
2. **Explain concept in one breath** — What BioCred does, simply
3. **Direct two audiences** — Agencies vs. Biologists with clear CTAs

---

## Sections

### 1. Hero

**Headline:** *"A structured registry of verified ecological experience"*

**Subtitle:** Translating documented evidence into a neutral experience distribution map.

**CTA Buttons:**
- **For Agencies** → View Example Profiles
- **For Biologists** → Create a Verified Profile

**Trust line:** *"Evidence-based. No rankings. No scores. No endorsements."*

---

### 2. The Problem — "The Resume Problem"

Three bullets:

1. CVs are **narrative**, not structured — experience alignment is context-dependent
2. Experience alignment is **context-dependent** — same role, different projects
3. Verification is **inconsistent** — claims vary in how they're documented

---

### 3. The Solution — "What BioCred Does"

**3-step visual:**

1. **Documentation Submitted** — Biologist uploads credentials and evidence
2. **Evidence Verified** — Reviewers confirm documentation
3. **Experience Distribution Rendered** — Neutral pentagon map produced

**Key sentence:** *"BioCred does not evaluate competence. It organizes documented evidence into a consistent, comparable format."*

---

### 4. Canonical Diagram Education

- Pentagon **without person** (no center marker)
- **"How to Read This"** explanation
- Educate visitors on interpreting the experience distribution map

---

### 5. Trust & Governance

- Verification process bullets
- Neutrality commitment bullets

---

### 6. For Whom

**Separate sections for each audience:**

| Audience | Value Props |
|----------|-------------|
| **Agency** | Find biologists with verified experience; compare scope; no narrative bias |
| **Biologist** | Document experience once; structured format; agency-ready profile |

---

### 7. Early Access

- **"Structured pilot mode"** messaging
- Buttons:
  - **Request Agency Access**
  - **Join as Founding Biologist**

---

### 8. Footer

- Neutrality statement
- Methodology link
- Data governance policy
- Contact
